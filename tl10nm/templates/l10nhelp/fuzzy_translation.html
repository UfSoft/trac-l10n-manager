Fuzzy Translation

Fuzzy entries, even if they account for translated entries for most other purposes, usually call for revision by the translator. Those may be produced when updating from older translated PO files according to a new PO template file, such that some new msgid has been modified only slightly out of an older one. The slight alteration in the original string (the msgid string) should often be reflected in the translated string, and this requires the intervention of the translator.
